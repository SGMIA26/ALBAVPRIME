Step 2 of 3 — Measurements

Seven numbers.
One perfect shirt.

Measure in inches. Follow each guide carefully. Your founding team personally reviews every order before fabric is cut.

01
Neck circumference
Tape around base of neck at Adam's apple. Add ½" for button comfort.
→ This number appears on your shirt tag (e.g. 15½")
02
Shoulder width
Tip to tip across upper back. The single most critical measurement.
→ 1cm error here shifts the full silhouette
03
Chest circumference
Around the fullest part of chest, across nipples. Breathe normally.
04
Waist / belly
Widest part of your midsection. Do not hold your breath.
05
Sleeve length
Shoulder tip to wrist bone where you want the cuff to sit.
→ Savile Row: cuff shows 1.5–2cm below your jacket sleeve
06
Bicep + Wrist
Widest bicep circumference + wrist bone circumference.
07
Shirt length
Neck base straight down to hem. Tucked-in style needs extra length.
